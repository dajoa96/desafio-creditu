About

Welcome to Kraken Race.

Kraken Games is a company dedicated to the development, edition and distribution of entertainment games for computers and advanced entertainment systems. We are a leader in the industry, proving to be creative and constantly innovating new quality products.

Kraken Race is a game that breaks all the rules. Thanks to our different game modes, each player can decide how he dares to challenge reality. A game for all dreamers, racers and rebels who want or not to follow the guidelines, everyone is free to choose and what to become. You can be a Track Racer, experiencing all the speed to the fullest. You can also run Street Races without rules where the first to arrive, no matter how, is the winner. There are also the Rally Races, where you will put all your senses to the test on the difficult roads and tracks against the clock.

This is a place to become a true Kraken Legend.
